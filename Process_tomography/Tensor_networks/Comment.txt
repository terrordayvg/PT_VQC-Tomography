Variational_MPO.jl or ipynb file, is the main.
It produces as output the fidelity of the comparison between the representation of U and MPO variational.
The methodology is based on the following article: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-38332-9

Requirements:
This code was implemented with the Julia programming language and combined with the Jupiter notebook enviroment.
Additional libraries needed: Pasta_Q - https://gtorlai.github.io/PastaQ.jl/dev/
                             ITensors - https://itensor.org/
                             Random - https://github.com/JuliaLang/julia/tree/master/stdlib/Random
                             Observers - https://github.com/GTorlai/Observers.jl
                             Optimisers: Optimisers - https://docs.sciml.ai/Optimization/stable/optimization_packages/optimisers/
                             LinearAlgebra - https://github.com/JuliaLang/julia/tree/master/stdlib/LinearAlgebra
                             SCS - https://github.com/jump-dev/SCS.jl
